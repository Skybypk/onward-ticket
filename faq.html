Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our flight reservation service.

About the Reservation

How do you manage to provide a verifiable reservation?

We book flight reservations through our accredited partners. The flight reservation is associated to a PNR (Passenger Name Record) which is verifiable on airlines websites.

How long is my reservation valid?

Reservations are typically valid for 3–7 days from booking, depending on the airline and route. You'll receive exact validity details with your reservation.

Does your booking include a ticket number?

No — this is a flight reservation (hold), not a purchased ticket. It includes a valid PNR you can verify on the airline's website, but not a ticket number.

Can I use this reservation for a visa application?

Yes, many of our customers use these reservations for visa applications and embassy interviews. The reservation is fully verifiable with a real PNR.

Payment & Refunds

How do I pay for my reservation?

We accept all major credit cards and PayPal. Payment is processed securely at checkout. Your payment details are never stored on our servers.

What is your refund policy?

If you don't receive your reservation within the stated timeframe, we offer a full refund. No questions asked. Simply contact our support team.

How much does a reservation cost?

Our reservations start from $14, depending on the route and type of reservation. You'll see the exact price at checkout before confirming.

Delivery

How quickly will I receive my reservation?

Most reservations are delivered within minutes during our working hours. In some cases it may take up to 30 minutes. If not received in time, you'll get a full refund.

How will the reservation be delivered?

You'll receive your reservation as a PDF via email. It includes your full itinerary and PNR details.
