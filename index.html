About Us

We built Onward Ticket because we were tired of being stuck at the boarding gate trying to book an emergency overpriced ticket. Onward Ticket is a platform that allows us and you to get a verifiable onward reservation from any country in the world. It's designed for travelers who need a quick, verifiable reservation while planning a trip. We are here to help you get the reservation details you need.

Our mission is to make travel as easy as possible for everyone. The reservation is a fully verifiable one with a valid PNR that respects the laws of the country of destination. We do not provide photoshopped reservations.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do you manage to provide verifiable reservation?

We book flight reservations through our accredited partners. The flight reservation is associated to a PNR (Passenger Name Record) which is verifiable on airlines websites.

How long is my reservation valid?

Reservations are typically valid for 3–7 days from booking, depending on the airline and route.

How do I pay for my reservation?

We accept all major credit cards and PayPal. Payment is processed securely at checkout.

What is your refund policy?

If you don't receive your reservation within the stated timeframe, we offer a full refund. No questions asked.

Does your booking include a ticket number?

No — this is a flight reservation (hold), not a purchased ticket. It includes a valid PNR but not a ticket number.
